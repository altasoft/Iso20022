Textual rule: CBPR_Electronic_Sequence_Number_TextualRule

For intra-day report: sequential number of the report, assigned by the account servicer. It is increased incrementally by 1 for each report sent electronically.
Note: it is recommended that it is not reset less than a period of a day.
Note: Some banks may not reset and will continue incrementally. This process is Bilaterally Determined
